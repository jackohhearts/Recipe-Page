Steak with Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Steak (Ribeye or Sirloin)
  • Potatoes (Sweet Potatoes Used Here)
  • Salad Greens
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Butter
  • Asparagus
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Salad Dressing of Choice

Instructions

  1. Salt steak in advance to let air dry uncovered in fridge.
  2. Roast oiled and sliced sweet potatoes at 400 degrees until cooked.
  3. Combine chopped parsley, garlic, red wine vinegar, and oil to create Chimmichuri sauce.
  4. Dress and prepare side salad.
  5. Roast asparagus until tender.
  6. Sear steak in cast iron until internal temperature reaches about 120F for medium rare.
  7. Let steak rest and then plate!
