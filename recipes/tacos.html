Braised Chuck Steak Tacos

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Red Onions
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Parsley
  • Oil
  • Lemon
  • Cilantro
  • Honey
  • Avocado
  • Chicken Stock
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Corn Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Blend cilantro, parsley, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt into sauce.
  2. Season and slow cook chuck roast overnight in slow cooker with stock.
  3. Slice red onions and soak in one part ACV and one part boiling water overnight in a jar.
  4. Shred cooked chuck roast with two forks.
  5. Plate and serve!
