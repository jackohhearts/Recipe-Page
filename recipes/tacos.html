Braised Chuck Steak Tacos

Ingredients

  • Skin on Salmon
  • Carrots
  • Beets
  • Parsnips
  • Tahini
  • Lemon
  • Bok Choy
  • Collard Greens
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Roast Parsnips and Carrots with a little bit of olive oil, salt, and peper.
  2. Roast beets in aluminum foil for roughly one hour.
  3. Prepare Tahini/Lemon sauce by whisking.
  4. Slice Bok Choy pretty.
  5. Steam collards until tender, and then season.
  6. Season salmon with salt, pepper, thyme.
  7. Pan fry salmon skin side down in oil until 80% done.
  8. Flip and cook a few more minutes.
  9. Plate and enjoy!
